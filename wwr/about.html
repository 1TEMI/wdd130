History

Founded in 2005 by a group of outdoor enthusiasts with a passion for adventure and a deep love for rivers, Water Way Rafting Company started as a small local outfitter offering guided rafting trips down the Osimini River. What began with just a few rafts and a handful of clients has grown into a premier rafting company known for its expertise, safety, and unforgettable experiences. Over the years, we’ve expanded to include more rivers, diverse trip options, and a dedicated team of experienced guides. Our commitment to adventure, nature, and community has made us a trusted name in the world of water rafting, welcoming thrill-seekers of all levels to experience the thrill of the rapids.
